FAQs

What types of jobs can you fill? ▼

We connect you with independent contractors in roles like:

  • Software Engineer (Junior/Mid/Senior)
  • Customer Support Engineer (L1/L2/L3)
  • Sales Representative (Sales Development/Inside Sales)
  • Accountant or Finance Associate

Check back often as our list keeps expanding.

Can you fill roles outside the listed niches? ▼

Absolutely! If you don't see the position you have in mind, just chat with our account managers. At Crossover, we create packages that fit your unique needs.

How can I hire through Crossover? ▼

You’ve got two great options:

1. Hire a Full-time Independent Contractor with Crossover

We tap into our global talent pool of highly vetted independent contractors in your niche. Using AI, we find your perfect match.

After interview and selection, we manage the full-time hiring contracts for you so that you don’t have to deal with paperwork.

2. Hire Your Own Full-time Employee

For our existing clients, you can use our talent pool without the extended Crossover services.

In this scenario, we’ll introduce you to your perfect match through our AI, and then you can hire them full-time, on your own terms.

Is Prime suitable for gig work or short-term projects? ▼

No, Prime is exclusively for full-time contractors looking for long-term commitments. We’re not a gig or short-term hub.

Our aim is to introduce you to the top 1% of contractors in your niche, so that you can add global talent to your ranks for the long haul.

Who will my company have a contractual relationship with? ▼

You’ll enter into a service agreement with Crossover, so that we can oversee the recruitment journey – from search and AI matching to workforce management.

You can ask your contractors to sign any additional agreements directly with your company, like NDAs.

How long does it take to hire with Crossover Prime? ▼

We expect you to be conducting interviews from a golden shortlist within the week. You'll have an offer on the table within the first 1-2 weeks, and 2-4 weeks following that your new team member will begin.

It's about speeding up your hiring journey and getting talent to the table – fast.

Where do your contractors come from? ▼

We’ve poured years into perfecting an AI-system that zeroes in on top candidates for tech roles.

We harness the power of LinkedIn and use localized ads make sure candidates from all over the world are included. No barriers, anyone from anywhere can apply.

Before making the cut, candidates have to pass a series of tests for cognitive ability, job-specific skills and English proficiency.

Testing is free, they only invest their time. Those who ace the tests are invited to join our global talent pool.

How do contract terminations work with Crossover? ▼

We champion smooth transitions, so we recommend a 2-week notice period to ensure a positive wrap-up for both sides, and to allow time for replacement plans.

But if the situation demands an immediate end, we accommodate same-day terminations – while always aiming for fairness.

Who's accountable if a contractor engages in misconduct? ▼

It’s important to clarify that our independent contractors aren’t Crossover employees. We streamline your tech team’s growth by facilitating recruitment and management.

While we aim for the best matches, mishaps can happen. For any contractor misconduct, the onus is on the individual contractor.

Crossover doesn’t shoulder liability or responsibility, except in cases arising from our own negligence or wilful misconduct.

What are Prime credits? ▼

Prime is free for existing clients based on the amount of credits they have with Crossover.

Credits are calculated on an individual basis and are unique to each client. They depend on other products or services your company is using within our partner network.

Credits can be exchanged for Prime usage fees, making it free to use. The service fees applied to hired contractors remain intact, but only kick in once you’ve found the ideal match.

How do contractors get paid? ▼

Simple! Once you find the best candidate, they sign a contract with us. We invoice you for the approved work hours, and manage the weekly contractor payments for you.
